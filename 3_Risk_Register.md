## Risk Rating Criteria

- Impact: Low / Medium / High  
- Likelihood: Low / Medium / High  
- Risk Level: Derived from Impact × Likelihood  

---

## Risk Lifecycle

1. Risk Identification  
2. Risk Assessment  
3. Risk Mitigation  
4. Risk Monitoring  
5. Risk Closure

## Business Value

This risk register enables:

- Centralized risk visibility  
- Better decision-making

Helps organizations proactively manage third-party risks and strengthen overall cybersecurity posture.
